WHY LYNK

What makes LYNK different.

Built to be taken seriously — by our users, by regulators, and by the infrastructure partners who move money with us.

10-second settlement

USDC on Solana handles cross-border settlement in under a second. Local fiat legs take another few seconds. Total delivery time: under 10 seconds, any African country to any other.

Regulated in Rwanda

Registered with the Rwanda Development Board. Engaging early with the National Bank of Rwanda regulatory sandbox. Compliance-first architecture from day one — AML, KYC, transaction monitoring built into the core, not bolted on.

Transparent unit economics

A flat 1.5% fee plus $0.99 per transaction. Mid-market FX rate published in real time. No hidden exchange rate spreads. What you see is what your recipient gets.

Payment Links — a category first

Generate a shareable URL that anyone in any country can pay. Sender pays in their currency. Recipient receives in theirs. No app download required. Built for WhatsApp, Instagram bios, invoices, freelance platforms.

Provider-agnostic infrastructure

Built with a provider abstraction layer from the ground up. Integrates with Yellow Card, Kotani Pay, Fonbnk, Paychant, and more. Route each transaction to the best available rail automatically. No single point of failure.

Africa-native, not Africa-adapted

Built in Kigali by Africans who've lived the problem. Launching on the Nigeria-Rwanda-Kenya corridor where we have the deepest user insight. Expanding to Ghana, Tanzania, Uganda, and South Africa within 18 months.
